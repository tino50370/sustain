OUR OFFER

Advisory & Technical Support

to strengthen environmental governance

We provide tailored advisory services to governments, institutions, and organisations seeking to strengthen environmental governance, strategy, and policy alignment.

Our services include:
  • Environmental policy development and review
  • Strategic planning for climate, land and biodiversity programs
  • Guidance on aligning national plans with UNFCCC, UNCCD, CBD, and other MEAs
  • Project design and proposal development

Research & Policy Analysis

to inform decision making

We produce evidence-based research to inform environmental decision-making and strengthen institutional capacity.

Focus areas:
  • Climate change policy analysis
  • Land degradation and drought assessments
  • Wetland and biodiversity studies
  • MEA negotiation tracking
  • Stakeholder consultations and surveys
  • Technical briefing papers and position documents

Events Management

from planning to execution

We provide full-service environmental and policy-focused event coordination for organisations seeking high-impact convenings.

Our events management services include:
  • Planning and hosting conferences, summits, and forums
  • Designing environmental themed events and technical workshops
  • Coordinating side events at COPs and multilateral conventions
  • Speaker and panel curation
  • Programme design and agenda development
  • Delegate management & logistics
  • Venue coordination, registration, and facilitation
  • Communications event branding, messaging, reporting

Capacity Building Programmes

Tailored for unique organisational needs

We design and provide tailored capacity-building programs designed to empower individuals and organisations for sustainable impact and growth.

Training areas include:
  • Climate governance & environmental policy literacy
  • MEA negotiation and participation (UNFCCC, CBD, UNCCD)
  • Environmental leadership and institutional strengthening
  • Scientific communication & policy engagement

Multilateral Environmental Agreement (MEA) Engagement

We support national, regional, and organisational participation in global environmental processes.

Support services include:
  • Pre-COP briefings and negotiation preparation
  • Technical guidance for delegations
  • Analysis of negotiation outcomes
  • Drafting statements, positions, and interventions
  • Organising side events at COPs, Climate Weeks, SBs
  • Facilitating stakeholder consultations before and after COPs
